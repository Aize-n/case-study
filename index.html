Case study

How a regional hotel group recovered their broken email & unified two domains under Google Workspace

A multi-property hospitality business in the Caucasus region. Two business domains, 13 staff mailboxes, and over 100 GB of historical email at risk of permanent loss. Client details withheld for confidentiality.

The situation before

Email was silently broken — and nobody knew.

Incoming mail on one domain had been failing silently for an unknown period — staff didn't realize messages were being lost

Mail infrastructure was fragmented across multiple providers, with one control panel completely inaccessible

One domain was blocked from Google Workspace due to a forgotten, abandoned configuration tied to it

Outbound email was being flagged as spam by every major provider — guest communications were unreliable

Over 100 GB of historical email across 13 mailboxes was at risk of permanent loss during any provider change

Full audit, recovery, migration, and documentation.

The outcome

Clean setup. Zero data loss. Zero downtime.

Both domains now run on a single Google Workspace tenant with one admin login. Staff continued working throughout — the migration ran entirely in the background.

Outbound deliverability fully restored. No more guest emails landing in spam.

Zero business disruption. Zero data loss.
